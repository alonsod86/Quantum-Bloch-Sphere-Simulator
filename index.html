Quantum Bloch-sphere Simulator

Simulate quantum states, both pure and mixed, in a Bloch-sphere with various quantum noise channels and gates. The effects of the various components are shown in real time on the right hand side in the Bloch-spheres.

Bloch-spheres

Two Bloch-spheres are shown here: one at the top and one at the bottom. The top sphere represents the Bloch-sphere without quantum noise influences, while the bottom will change shape depending on the noise channel applied. A noise-free Bloch-sphere has a radius of 1, pure are generally on the surface of the sphere, while mixed states generally are inside the sphere.

Quantum states

States can be created in the upper left section, either by entering the density matrix values or by adjusting the φ and θ sliders.>
